### Vendor Cash Discount (vendor_discount_calculated)

* **Description**: This field calculates the vendor cash discount amount by multiplying the amount due by the discount percentage. For example, if the amount due is $100 and the discount is 2%, this field will calculate $2.00 as the discount amount. The result is rounded to 2 decimal places.

* **Field type**: formula

* **Data type**: number

* **Required**: false

* **Exported**: false

* **Hidden**: true
