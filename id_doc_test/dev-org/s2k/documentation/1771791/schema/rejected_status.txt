### Reject Credit Memo (rejected_status)

- **Description**: This field allows users to specify whether a credit memo should be rejected and sent to the NON-S2K Inbox or take no action. When set to "Send to NON-S2K Inbox", the credit memo will be routed to a separate workflow. When set to "-- no action --", the credit memo will continue through the normal processing flow.

- **Field type**: data

- **Data type**: enum (string values)

- **Required**: No

- **Exported**: No

- **Hidden**: No
