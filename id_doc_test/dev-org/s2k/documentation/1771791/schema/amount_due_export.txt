### Invoice Amt. (S2K export) (amount_due_export)

* **Description**: This field calculates the total invoice amount by taking the subtotal and adding various charges (miscellaneous charges, freight, dropship freight, merchant tax, retention tax, plate charge, pallet charge) while subtracting any discounts (trade discount, purchase discount, backhaul allowance, rebate). The result represents the final amount due for export to the S2K system.

* **Field type**: formula

* **Data type**: number

* **Required**: false

* **Exported**: true

* **Hidden**: false
