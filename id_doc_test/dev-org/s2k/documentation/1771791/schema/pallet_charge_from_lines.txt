### pallet_charge_from_lines (pallet_charge_from_lines)

* **Description**: This field stores a numerical value representing pallet charges calculated from line items on the invoice. It's a hidden datapoint that automatically aggregates pallet-related charges from the document's line items.

* **Field type**: data

* **Data type**: number

* **Required**: No

* **Exported**: No

* **Hidden**: Yes
