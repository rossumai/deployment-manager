### misc_charges_from_lines (misc_charges_from_lines)

* **Description**: This field stores the total value of miscellaneous charges calculated from line items on the invoice. It's a hidden field that isn't directly visible to users but works behind the scenes to track these charges.

* **Field type**: data

* **Data type**: number

* **Required**: No

* **Exported**: No

* **Hidden**: Yes
