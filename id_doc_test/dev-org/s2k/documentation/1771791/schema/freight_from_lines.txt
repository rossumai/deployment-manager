### freight_from_lines (freight_from_lines)

- **Description:** This field stores the total freight amount calculated from line items on the invoice. It's a hidden field that's not directly editable by users and doesn't appear in the interface. The value is automatically computed and used internally by the system.

- **Field type:** data

- **Data type:** number

- **Required:** No

- **Exported:** No

- **Hidden:** Yes
